💸 Expense

Record money you spend. Decreases your wallet balance.

  • You can optionally select a category
  • Link to a budget to track spending limits
  • Budget progress updates automatically

🏦 Saving

Move money toward your savings goals.

  • You must select a savings goal
  • Money is deducted from wallet and added to goal
  • Creates a 'saving' type transaction

📌 Category (Optional)

Categories help organize your transactions but are not required.

  • You can leave category empty
  • Only needed if you want budget tracking
  • Manage categories from Settings
